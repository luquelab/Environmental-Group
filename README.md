# Environmental-Group

A free and beginner-friendly bioinformatics website designed to make real bioinformatics simple, clear, and accessible.

Here, users can explore biological sequence data, study important sequence properties, compare proteins or genes, and visualize results in an easy way. The goal is to make bioinformatics feel less confusing and more approachable, even for people who are completely new to it.

## What you can do here

- Work with biological sequences
- Explore sequence properties
- Compare proteins or genes
- Visualize results
- Learn bioinformatics in a simpler way

Even if you do not know the terms yet, this website is built to help you start.
